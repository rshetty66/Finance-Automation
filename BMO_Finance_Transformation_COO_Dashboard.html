🎯 COO Strategic Imperative — Top 3 Actions
Automate the core: R2R ($15.6M, 49.5% of spend) has the highest automation density — 65–70% of transactional R2R activities (JE, reconciliation, close) are automation-ready, representing $7–9M in savings.
Rationalize the 14.84% "Other" spend: $4.7M in ad hoc, training, and administrative activity is the highest-risk category — elimination and AI-assisted copilot can reduce this by 40–50% in Phase 1.
Redesign service delivery: 42.25% Corporate-delivered activity ($13.3M) should be redistributed toward SSC/BPO and CoE models, reducing unit cost from $144K/FTE toward the $86K–$100K benchmark.
