Executive Summary

Sprott's finance function faces critical operational risk: 100% manual JE processing, 19% rec automation, and 56-day average close lateness create $8.8M–$12.7M in annual waste. A four-phase, 18-month transformation targeting SAP automation, reconciliation platform deployment, and continuous close redesign will deliver $10M+ annual savings and material SOX/regulatory risk reduction. Immediate action on governance remediation and quick wins can generate $2M+ in savings within 90 days.

TRANSFORMATION THEMES
Automate the Record-to-Report Engine High
Eliminate manual JE processing via SAP auto-posting, ML classification, and auto-accrual rules.
Deploy Intelligent Reconciliation Platform High
Replace Excel-based recs with BlackLine auto-certification, IC matching hub, and SLA workflows.
Redesign Close Cycle to Continuous Accounting Medium
Shift from batch close mentality to distributed activity model eliminating structural overtime.
