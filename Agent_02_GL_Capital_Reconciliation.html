⬡ Agent Role in BCAR Architecture

Agent 02 is the foundational bridge in the capital reporting chain. Every other agent depends on the CET1 baseline this agent produces. It performs the most complex, high-risk step in the BCAR process — translating IFRS accounting into OSFI regulatory capital.

INPUT:SAP S/4HANA trial balance · BlackLine reconciled positions · Capital Engine outputs
OUTPUT:CET1/T1/Total Capital waterfall · OSFI Workpaper · Break analysis · CRO dashboard data
DOWNSTREAM:Feeds Agent 04 (Capital Calc), Agent 08 (Analytics), Agent 12 (Submission Quality)

S/4HANA trial balance exported via SAP BW/SE16N → loaded into Excel workbook → 15 deductions calculated manually with hardcoded OSFI parameters → BlackLine reconciliation reviewed separately → manually reconciled back to capital position. T+6, 30+ hrs, 4 spreadsheet owners.
Future State (AWS Bedrock)
S/4HANA GL API triggers agent at period close → BlackLine rec status auto-ingested → all 15 deductions calculated using OSFI Knowledge Base parameters → CET1 waterfall generated with full BCBS 239 lineage. T+1, 3.8 hrs, zero spreadsheets.
Why This Agent Is Critical: The GL-to-Capital bridge is where most OSFI findings originate. Manual GL account mapping errors, stale OSFI parameter tables, and unreconciled BlackLine positions have historically caused CET1 restatements. AWS Bedrock agent with an OSFI CAR 2024 Knowledge Base eliminates all three root causes.

# Deduction OSFI CAR Ref S/4HANA GL Range Calculation Logic Complexity Illust. ($M) AI Point
D01Goodwill
Net of associated deferred tax liability		§2.1.4116xxxFull deduction. Goodwill balance less DTL that would arise from unwinding (if any). Includes goodwill embedded in equity method investments.LOW($6,200)AI-01
D02Other Intangibles
Excluding MSR — net of DTL		§2.1.5117xxxFull deduction. Purchased and internally developed intangibles excluding mortgage servicing rights. Agent identifies and excludes MSR sub-accounts automatically.MEDIUM($3,800)AI-01
D03Debit Valuation Adjustment (DVA)
Own-credit risk MTM on derivatives		§2.1.6431xxxDeduct cumulative unrealized gain attributable to own credit risk deterioration in derivative fair values. Can be negative (add-back) if own credit has improved.MEDIUM($180)AI-01
D04Cash Flow Hedge Reserve
Unrealized AOCI from CFH derivatives		§2.1.7322xxxRemove from CET1 the unrealized gains/losses on cash flow hedging instruments recorded in AOCI. Both positive and negative amounts are excluded — this is a filter, not solely a deduction.MEDIUM($420)AI-01
D05Pension Net Asset
IAS 19 DB plan surplus		§2.1.8123xxxDeduct net pension asset (prepaid pension) net of associated DTL. Only applies when DB plan is in surplus. If in deficit, pension liability is already on balance sheet — no additional deduction.LOW($95)AI-01
D06DTA — Tax Loss Carryforwards
Full deduction, no threshold		§2.1.9136xxx100% deduction from CET1. DTAs arising from unused tax losses and unused tax credits — full deduction regardless of amount. No netting against DTLs permitted under OSFI CAR.LOW($210)AI-02
D07DTA — Temporary Differences
⚑ THRESHOLD — 10% / 17.65% Combined		§2.1.10136xxxBelow 10% of CET1: risk-weighted 250%. Above 10% (individual) AND above 17.65% (combined with D11 + D12): deducted from CET1. Requires iterative calculation — CET1 denominator changes as deductions applied.HIGH($1,240)AI-02
D08AFS Unrealized Gains Filter
Equity securities only		§2.1.11321xxxRemove unrealized gains on AFS equity securities from CET1 (losses remain in CET1). Applies to equity instruments at FVOCI under IFRS 9. Fixed income AFS unrealized gains/losses are included in CET1.MEDIUM($120)AI-01
D09Securitization Gain on Sale
Excess spread / GOS		§2.1.12475xxxDeduct gain on sale from securitization transactions and associated increases in equity (e.g., residual interests, excess spread). Designed to prevent recognition of future income as current capital.MEDIUM($85)AI-01
D10IRB Expected Loss Shortfall
⚑ CROSS-AGENT — Capital Engine Required		§2.1.13Capital EngineWhen IRB Expected Loss (PD×LGD×EAD) exceeds IFRS 9 Stage 1+2 provisions: 50% shortfall deducted from CET1, 50% from T2. Agent 02 calls Agent 05 (IRB/EL Agent) via API to get this figure.HIGH($580)AI-03
D11Significant Investments in Financial Entities
⚑ THRESHOLD — 10% / 17.65% Combined		§2.1.14151xxx>10% ownership or significant influence. Below 10% individual threshold AND below 17.65% combined threshold (with D07+D12): risk-weighted at 250%. Above thresholds: full CET1 deduction.HIGH($890)AI-02
D12Mortgage Servicing Rights (MSR)
✓ BELOW THRESHOLD — No Deduction This Quarter		§2.1.14118xxxMSR below 10% individual threshold (CET1 basis) and below 17.65% combined threshold. Risk-weighted at 250% for below-threshold amount. Agent 02 monitors quarterly and flags if threshold approaches.HIGH—AI-02
D13Minority Interest (Non-Qualifying)
Excess over subsidiary capital req		§2.1.15385xxxRemove non-controlling interest capital in excess of the amount needed to support the subsidiary's risk-weighted assets. Qualifying minority interest (within subsidiary capital requirements) is included.MEDIUM($75)AI-01
D14Reciprocal Cross-Holdings
Mutual holdings inflating capital		§2.1.16151xxxFull deduction for cross-shareholdings between financial institutions designed to mutually inflate capital bases. Agent identifies based on OSFI-flagged counterparty list cross-referenced with investment register.LOW($45)AI-01
D15Investment in Unconsolidated Financial Subsidiaries
Outside regulatory scope		§2.1.17154xxxInvestment in financial entities not included in the regulatory consolidation scope. Deducted to prevent counting of capital in entities that are separately regulated or below OSFI consolidation threshold.MEDIUM($565)AI-01
